## **Reporting Period:** 2026-03-18 - 2025-03-24

I was in AsiaBSDCon in this reporting week. I continued working on LLDB in my free time. So far:

- ProcessFreeBSDKernelCore is almost done. Waiting for reviews.
- DynamicLoaderFreeBSDKernel is halfway there. If reviewing ProcessFreeBSDKernelCore takes too long, I'll create DynamicLoader
  PR along with it.
- olce@ and I looked at the intelhfi patch series. Although it looks good, we concluded that using cpu_group isn't a good choice over pcpu.
  Feedback sent to the author.
- We also discussed on HMP scheduling.
- While waiting for LLDB reviewers, I'll work on foundation grant stuff and AsiaBSDCon wrap-up articles.
- I fixed a bug that disabled LLVM (e.g. clang, lldb) color mode over SSH on Ghostty, Alacritty, and kitty. Waiting for reviewers.
