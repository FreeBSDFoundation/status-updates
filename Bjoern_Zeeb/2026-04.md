### Maintaining wireless drivers for Linux v7.0
#### **Overall Status:** Done
#### **Summary**

LinuxKPI based wireless drivers are tracking recent Linux kernel versions.
In February we managed to get v6.19 based drivers in and while the plan
was to wait a bit longer before updating again it turned out that v7.0
would be an easy update with little churn.

Ignoring build times, this took about a good working day in total
for all 8 drivers:

```

athk-c   2 files changed, 5 insertions(+), 5 deletions(-)

ath11k  27 files changed, 1999 insertions(+), 110 deletions(-)
```

### Project: chandef / chanctx rework for Realtek and Mediatek
#### **Overall Status:** Done
#### **Summary**

A while ago Linux introduced an emulation for driver calldowns
to set the chandef channel information based on the chanctx.
We had a the KPI for the emulation as the drivers require it.
It turned out not to be sophisticated enough.
In addition our chanctx management needed chaning in preparation
for supporting multiple chanctx in the future.
