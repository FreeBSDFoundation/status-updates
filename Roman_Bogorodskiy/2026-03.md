### Project: libvirt on FreeBSD
#### **Overall Status:** On Track
#### **Summary**

Merged the libvirt `nodeinfo` improvements.

Merged the NUMA and related documentation updates.

Implemented and merged the libvirt `nodecpumap` FreeBSD support.

Updated the libvirt port to enable BHYVE on arm64.

Opened a virt-manager [PR](https://github.com/virt-manager/virt-manager/pull/1061)

to get memory usage within the guest. Probably will need to look more into it.

Opened another virt-manager [PR](https://github.com/virt-manager/virt-manager/pull/1063)
to enable connecting to the nmdm consoles.
